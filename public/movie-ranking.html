The Best Movies According to Aaryan.

My top 3 movies of all time.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This drama tries to entertain while delivering thought-provoking messages on the value of life, frienships, and relationships.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a timeless sci-fi classic. With his signature narrative structure, editing, and visual language, Christopher Nolan brings an unprecedented sense of beauty and philosophical depth to the space film genre.

3 Idiots!

"3 Idiots" teaches valuable life lessons, encouraging viewers to prioritize passion and learning over societal pressures and rote memorization.
